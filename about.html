Meet the Doctor

Dr. Drew Randall



At his practice in Dallas, TX, Dr. Randall provides a variety of general, restorative, periodontal, cosmetic, and advanced dental treatments. With his vast experience and continued training, he utilizes innovative techniques, such as BioRejuvenation dentistry. Through cosmetic dental procedures, Dr. Randall is able to enhance and/or restore the appearance of his patients’ smiles. For patients requiring a restorative or periodontal treatment, Dr. Randall also provides dental bridges, crowns, inlays, onlays, implants, root canal therapy, and scaling and root planing. Additionally, at Randall Dentistry, he offers sedation dentistry to allow for more comfortable and relaxing treatments.
‍
After growing up in Irving, TX, Dr. Randall attended Southern Methodist University. He then went on to attend Texas A&M University System – Baylor College of Dentistry in Dallas, TX from 1994 – 1998. Dr. Randall completed his advanced education in general dentistry at the VCU School of Dentistry in Richmond, VA before joining his father, Dr. Warren Randall, at Randall and Randall Dental Associates in Lewisville, TX. In 2009, Dr. Randall started Randall Dentistry as his own private practice in Dallas.
‍
In his spare time, Dr. Randall enjoys spending time with his beautiful wife and their four children. They are also members of Park Cities Presbyterian Church.

About Us

Conveniently located on Hillcrest Avenue in Dallas, TX, Randall Dentistry is well-equipped with an experienced dental team, innovative technology, and spacious treatment rooms. Dr. Drew Randall and our staff have formulated our facility with our patients in mind in order to allow them to have a positive and comfortable experience with each visit to our practice. Upon arrival, patients will be greeted, guided through the check-in process, and led to our relaxing reception area. We understand that time is precious, which is why we make every effort to make waiting times as short as possible. In our treatment rooms, Dr. Randall uses modern technology that allows for more comprehensive and accurate diagnoses and dental procedures for the benefit of his patients. We also offer several sedation dentistry options to allow our patients to feel comfortable and relaxed during their treatments. To learn more about what we have to offer at our Dallas facility, we invite you to view our complete list of dental services performed by Dr. Randall.
